Privacy Policy

Introduction

Welcome to Claim Lah. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our expense tracking application, in compliance with Malaysia's Personal Data Protection Act 2010 (PDPA).

Information We Collect

Personal Information

When you register for Claim Lah, we collect:

  • Name and email address (for account creation)

Expense Data

To provide our expense tracking services, we store:

  • Expense descriptions, amounts, dates, and categories
  • Receipt images (Plus tier only)
  • Export history and report preferences

Technical Data

We automatically collect:

  • Device information (browser type, operating system)
  • IP address and location data (for security)
  • Login timestamps and session data

How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Provide our services: Track expenses, generate reports, and manage your account
  • Process payments: Handle subscription upgrades and billing (Plus tier)
  • Improve our app: Analyze usage patterns to enhance features and user experience
  • Communicate with you: Send important updates, security alerts, and support responses
  • Ensure security: Detect fraud, prevent abuse, and protect your account

Sharing Your Information

We do NOT sell your personal data. We only share information with:

  • Service providers: Cloud hosting, payment processing (Stripe), analytics tools
  • Business transfers: In case of merger, acquisition, or sale of assets (you will be notified)

All third-party providers are contractually obligated to protect your data and comply with PDPA.

Your Rights Under PDPA

Under Malaysia's Personal Data Protection Act 2010, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Withdraw consent: Stop receiving marketing communications
  • Data portability: Export your expense data in CSV format

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via:

  • Email notification to your registered address
  • In-app notification banner

Continued use of Claim Lah after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
